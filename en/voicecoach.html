🔧 For the curious: how it works insideThe analysis pipeline, explained — only if you feel like diving in▾

All processing happens on the device: no third-party AI services, no audio, text or data sent out, nothing stored on external servers.

Capture and feature extraction. Multiple dimensions of the voice are processed in real time: variability, rhythm, coherence and pitch behavior.
Contextual interpretation. Acoustic signals are combined with an interpretation engine that tells main from secondary topics to place the reading in context.
Feedback generation. The detected state is translated into short, natural coach phrases across areas like work, purpose and relationships.
Adaptive system. With use, the app spots recurring patterns over time and adjusts its readings to your way of speaking.
